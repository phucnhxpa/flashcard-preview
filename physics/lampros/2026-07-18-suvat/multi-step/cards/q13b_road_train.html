Source status: Officially controlled Pearson homework item.
Recall promptA car accelerates at 3.7 m s⁻² from 30 m s⁻¹ while passing a stationary road train. It reaches 130 km h⁻¹ as it passes the cab. Find the train length.
Concept sketch: derived from the method stated on this card.

Method

STEP 1/3Convert before squaring

130 km h⁻¹ = 130/3.6 = 36.111… m s⁻¹.

STEP 2/3Choose the no-time equation

Use v²=u²+2as because time is not needed. Rearrange s=(v²−u²)/(2a).

STEP 3/3Keep precision until the end

s=[36.111…²−30²]/[2(3.7)] =54.596… m, then round final length to 55 m.

Recall answer: 54.596… m ≈54.6 m; official result 55 m.
