Source status: Officially controlled answer; full arithmetic is a derived calculation from the controlled values.
Recall promptAn object is released from rest. Find the distance travelled from t=0.20 s to t=0.30 s, neglecting air resistance.
Concept sketch: derived from the method stated on this card.

Method

STEP 1/3Keep one clock

This is not a fresh 0.10 s motion: at 0.20 s the object already has non-zero velocity.

STEP 2/3Find endpoint positions

With downward positive, s(t)=½gt². Compute s(0.30) and s(0.20) from release.

STEP 3/3Subtract later minus earlier

Δs = ½(9.81)[0.30² − 0.20²] = 0.24525 m ≈ 0.25 m.

Recall answer: 0.24525 m ≈ 0.25 m, option B.
