Recall promptA ball is projected vertically upward from s=0 at speed u. How long is it above height h₀?
Concept sketch: derived from the method stated on this card.

Method

STEP 1/3Set one sign convention

Take upward positive, so a=−g for the whole flight and h₀=ut−½gt².

STEP 2/3Form the quadratic

Rearrange: ½gt²−ut+h₀=0. Its roots are the upward and downward crossing times.

STEP 3/3Use physical root order

Time above = t_later−t_earlier. Check 0<h₀<u²/(2g) for two positive crossings; otherwise interpret the physical domain before subtracting.

Recall answer: Derived extension: time above h₀ = 2√(u²−2gh₀)/g, provided two relevant physical crossings exist.
