Q004 · Area under a non-negative v–t graph

Source status: Source-controlled Q8 / PMT Q11; 1 mark. Secure answer, not a claim that a mark scheme is present.
Recall promptv rises 0→20 m s⁻¹ in 0–2 s, then remains 20 m s⁻¹ to 4 s. Find total distance.
Concept sketch: derived from the graph method on this card.

Reveal the method

STEP 1/3Identify the quantity
Velocity is non-negative throughout, so signed area and distance have the same magnitude.
STEP 2/3Split the shape
Triangle: ½×2×20 = 20 m. Rectangle: 2×20 = 40 m.
STEP 3/3Add and check units
20 + 40 = 60 m. (m s⁻¹)×s gives m.
Recall answer: 60 m (option C).
