Recall promptFrom free fall to an opened parachute, how should acceleration change?
Concept sketch: derived from the graph method on this card.

Reveal the method

STEP 1/3Use the decisive wording
“Falls freely” means initial drag is ignored, so acceleration is constant gravitational acceleration.
STEP 2/3Model parachute opening
Opening produces a large force opposite the downward motion, so acceleration reverses sign under a downward-positive convention.
STEP 3/3Finish at terminal motion
As the new terminal speed is approached, net force falls to zero and so does acceleration.
Recall answer: Initially constant downward g; a large acceleration opposite the downward motion on opening; then acceleration tends to zero at the new terminal speed.
Diagnostic / redo: If you began with gradual drag while the prompt says free fall, underline the modelling phrase first.
