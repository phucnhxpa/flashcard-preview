Core Implicit Differentiation Technique

5 moves
The fundamental procedure of differentiating an implicit equation F(x,y)=0 term-by-term, applying chain and product rules to attach dy/dx to every y-term.
Differentiate implicitly to obtain dy/dx without isolating y
$\;x^2 + xy + y^2 = 7\;$
$\;2x + y + x\dfrac{dy}{dx} + 2y\dfrac{dy}{dx} = 0 \Rightarrow \dfrac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{-(2x+y)}{x+2y}\;$
14q implicit differentiation

13q implicit differentiation
