Arithmetic Simplification

1 moves · 1 examples
Cancel shared powers of 2 between coefficient and denominator before multiplying
$\;\binom{6}{r} \cdot 2^{6-r} \cdot \left(\dfrac{x}{4}\right)^r = \binom{6}{r} \cdot \dfrac{2^{6-r}}{4^r} \cdot x^r\;$
