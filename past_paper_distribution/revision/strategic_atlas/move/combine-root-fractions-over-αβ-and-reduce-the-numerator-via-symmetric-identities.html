  2. Rules out solving the quadratic explicitly for Оұ and ОІ, since the symmetric structure means coefficients suffice.

  4. Bridges to the first operation: write the sum as (numerator)/(ОұОІ), where the numerator is a power sum like ОұВІ+ОІВІ.

Mechanic вҖ” Concrete operations

  1. Identify Оұ+ОІ and ОұОІ from the quadratic's coefficients via Vieta.
  2. Rewrite the fractional expression with common denominator ОұОІ (or (ОұОІ)^k as appropriate).

Worked Micro-Example

If Оұ, ОІ are roots of $2x^2+5x+7=0$, then $\alpha+\beta=-\tfrac{5}{2}$, $\alpha\beta=\tfrac{7}{2}$. Then $\tfrac{2\alpha}{\beta}+\tfrac{2\beta}{\alpha}=\tfrac{2(\alpha^2+\beta^2)}{\alpha\beta}=\tfrac{2((\alpha+\beta)^2-2\alpha\beta)}{\alpha\beta}=\tfrac{2(\tfrac{25}{4}-7)}{\tfrac{7}{2}}=\tfrac{-3/2}{7/2}=-\tfrac{3}{7}.$
