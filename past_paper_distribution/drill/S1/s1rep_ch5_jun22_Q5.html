Find k, E(R), E(R²), Var(R) and standard deviation for a discrete RV

Question

The discrete random variable $R$ has probability distribution:

$r$12345
$P(R=r)$$0.10$$0.20$$k$$0.25$$0.15$

(a) Find the value of $k$.   (b) Find $E(R)$.   (c) Find $E(R^2)$.   (d) Find $\text{Var}(R)$ and the standard deviation of $R$.

[14 marks]

(a) [2 marks] · 1 step

STEP 1/1

Formula: $\sum P(R = r) = 1$

$0.10 + 0.20 + k + 0.25 + 0.15 = 1$

$0.70 + k = 1$

$\boxed{k = 0.30}$

(b) [3 marks] · 1 step

$k = 0.30$
STEP 1/1

Formula: $E(R) = \sum r \cdot P(R=r)$

$E(R) = 1(0.10) + 2(0.20) + 3(0.30) + 4(0.25) + 5(0.15)$

$= 0.10 + 0.40 + 0.90 + 1.00 + 0.75$

$\boxed{E(R) = 3.15}$

(c) [3 marks] · 1 step

$E(R) = 3.15$
STEP 1/1

Formula: $E(R^2) = \sum r^2 \cdot P(R=r)$

$E(R^2) = 1^2(0.10) + 2^2(0.20) + 3^2(0.30) + 4^2(0.25) + 5^2(0.15)$

$= 0.10 + 0.80 + 2.70 + 4.00 + 3.75$

$\boxed{E(R^2) = 11.35}$

(d) [3 marks] · 1 step

$E(R) = 3.15,\; E(R^2) = 11.35$
Carry-in from previous step$E(R)=3.15,\; E(R^2)=11.35$
STEP 1/1

Formula: $\text{Var}(R) = E(R^2) - [E(R)]^2$

$\text{Var}(R) = 11.35 - (3.15)^2 = 11.35 - 9.9225$

Must SQUARE the mean: $(3.15)^2 = 9.9225$ — common error is to forget this step

$\text{Var}(R) = 1.4275$

$\text{SD}(R) = \sqrt{1.4275} \approx 1.195$

$\boxed{\text{Var}(R) = 1.4275,\; \text{SD} \approx 1.20}$
