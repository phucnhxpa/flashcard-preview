Standalone question

The bus is on time with probability 0.6 (late 0.4). If on time she catches the train with probability 0.8; if late, with probability 0.6. Find P(bus on time | catches train). Official answer: E, 2/3.

Concept visual - supports the stated method; it does not add a different question or solution.

Multi-step working

Step 1 - Complete the tree (both stages)

\(\text{on time }(0.6):\ \text{catch }0.48,\ \text{miss }0.12;\qquad\text{late }(0.4):\ \text{catch }0.24,\ \text{miss }0.16.\)

Step 2 - Total probability of catching

\(P(\text{catch})=0.48+0.24=0.72.\)

Step 3 - Conditional formula

\(P(\text{on time}\mid\text{catch})=\frac{0.48}{0.72}=\frac{2}{3}.\)

Recall answer: P(on time | catches) = 0.48/0.72 = 2/3, answer E. Do not assume independence: catching the train depends on the bus being on time, so read 0.8 / 0.6 off the tree.
