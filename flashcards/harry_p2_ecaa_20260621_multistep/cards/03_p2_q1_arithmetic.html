Standalone question

The sequence 78 + 75 + 72 + ... . (a) Find the 18th term (2). (b) Find the sum of the first 100 terms (2).

Concept visual - supports the stated method; it does not add a different question or solution.

Multi-step working

Step 1 - Identify a and d

\(a=78,\quad d=-3.\)

Step 2 - 18th term (supplied formula)

\(u_{18}=a+17d=78+17(-3)=78-51=27.\)

Step 3 - Sum of 100 terms (supplied formula)

\(S_{100}=\tfrac{100}{2}\bigl[2(78)+99(-3)\bigr]=50(156-297)=-7050.\)

Recall answer: u18 = 27 and S100 = -7050. The terms decrease by 3, so a large negative sum is expected.
