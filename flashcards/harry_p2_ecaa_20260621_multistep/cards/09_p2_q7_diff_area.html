Concept visual - supports the stated method; it does not add a different question or solution.

Multi-step working

Step 1 - (a)(b) Derivative and k

\(\frac{dy}{dx}=3x^2-10x^{3/2}-\tfrac{k}{2}x^{-1/2}+28;\ \text{at }x=9\text{ setting it to zero gives }k=6.\)

Step 2 - (c) Exact area then 3 s.f.

\(\text{Exact shaded area}=\tfrac{6939}{28}\approx 247.821\Rightarrow 248\ (\text{3 s.f.}).\)

Step 3 - (c) Sign sense-check

\(\text{A negative answer flags a sign slip: here curve}-\text{line has the curve above the line, so the integral is positive}.\)
