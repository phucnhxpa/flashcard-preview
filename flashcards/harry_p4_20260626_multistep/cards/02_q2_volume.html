Standalone question

The final guide identifies P4 Q2 as: y = (5/3)√(x−1), for 2 ≤ x ≤ 8, rotated through 2π about the x-axis. Find the volume of revolution.

Concept visual - supports the stated method; it does not add a different question or solution.

Multi-step working

Step 1 - Choose the required P4 formula

\(V=\pi\int_a^b y^2\,dx.\)

Step 2 - Substitute the curve

\(y^2=\tfrac{25}{9}(x-1),\quad V=\pi\int_2^8\tfrac{25}{9}(x-1)\,dx.\)

Step 3 - Integrate

\(V=\tfrac{25\pi}{9}\left[\tfrac12(x-1)^2\right]_2^8=\tfrac{25\pi}{18}(49-1).\)

Recall answer: V = 200π/3 cubic units. The required P4 form is π∫y²dx; π∫x²dy is not required for P4.
