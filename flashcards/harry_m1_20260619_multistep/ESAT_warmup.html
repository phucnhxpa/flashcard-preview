Carry-in / given: “Difference less than 1” means the size of the gap is under 1.
Algebra-only: no spatial, graphical, or statistical diagram is needed for this reasoning.

Write the gap as one modulus statement and unfold it on a number line; keep every term positive before squaring.

Formula: \(\lvert 2\sqrt{n}-7\rvert<1\ \Rightarrow\ -1<2\sqrt{n}-7<1\).

Add 7: \(6<2\sqrt{n}<8\).
