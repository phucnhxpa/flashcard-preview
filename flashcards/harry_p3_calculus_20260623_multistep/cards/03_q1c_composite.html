Standalone question

For the same functions, find fg(0).

Concept visual - supports the stated method; it does not add a different question or solution.

Multi-step working

Step 1 - Evaluate g(0)

\(g(0)=\frac{5(0)+3}{0+2}=\frac{3}{2}.\)

Step 2 - Compose with f

\(fg(0)=f\!\left(\tfrac32\right)=\ln\!\left(\left(\tfrac32\right)^2+3\right).\)

Step 3 - Simplify

\(\left(\tfrac32\right)^2+3=\frac94+3=\frac{21}{4}.\)

Recall answer: fg(0) = ln(21/4).
