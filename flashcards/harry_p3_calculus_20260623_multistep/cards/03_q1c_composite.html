Q1(c) — composite value

Question

Official prompt. For $f(x)=\ln(x^2+3)$ and $g(x)=\dfrac{5x+3}{x+2}$, find $fg(0)$.

Status: COMPLETED W/ PROMPTING — inserted out of order before Q1(d) was deferred.

[2 marks]

Part (c)[2 marks] · 2 steps

STEP 1/2M1

Evaluate the inner function first:

$$g(0)=\frac{3}{2}.$$

STEP 2/2A1

$$fg(0)=f\!\left(\frac32\right)=\ln\!\left(\frac94+3\right)=\boxed{\ln\!\left(\frac{21}{4}\right)}.$$

Target: $\boxed{\ln(21/4)}$
