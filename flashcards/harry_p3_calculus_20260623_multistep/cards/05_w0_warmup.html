W0 — quadratic meets a line

Question

The curve $y=x^2+ax+b$ meets $y=x+1$ at $x=2$ and $x=4$. Find $a$ and $b$.

Status: LESSON EVIDENCE — PAPER UNRESOLVED; COMPLETED W/ PROMPTING. No official identity or marks are assigned.

No official marks

Warm-up4 steps

STEP 1/4

At each meeting point, set the two $y$-values equal:

$$x^2+ax+b=x+1.$$

STEP 2/4

At $x=2$: $4+2a+b=3$, hence $2a+b=-1$.

STEP 3/4

At $x=4$: $16+4a+b=5$, hence $4a+b=-11$.

STEP 4/4

Subtract: $2a=-10$, so $a=-5$ and $b=9$.

Target: $\boxed{a=-5,\ b=9}$
