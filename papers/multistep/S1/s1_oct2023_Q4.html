Mistake guard from the attempt/revision notes.

What went wrong. Two failures: (b) arithmetic with non-matching denominators got tangled; (d) Phuc didn't translate "$Y < 20$" into a condition on $X$ first, so he tried to compute a 2-D joint distribution that doesn't exist (since $Y$ is a function of $X$, there is no independence to invoke).

Phuc's incorrect approach (annotated). For (d): Phuc summed products like $P(X=x)\cdot P(Y=y)$ as if $X$ and $Y$ were independent. They are not --- $Y = 30/X$ is a deterministic function of $X$. The correct move is "$Y < 20 \iff X > 1.5 \iff X \in {2,3,4}$".

STEP 2/5Part (b)

Strategy. Active recall: choose the method for Part (b) before unmasking. Reproduce exam-safe working, not just the final answer.

(b) Compute $E(1/X^2)$:
$$E!\left(\tfrac{1}{X^2}\right) = 1\cdot\tfrac{1}{10} + \tfrac{1}{4}\cdot\tfrac{1}{5} + \tfrac{1}{9}\cdot\tfrac{3}{10} + \tfrac{1}{16}\cdot\tfrac{2}{5}.$$

Terms:
- $1 \cdot \tfrac{1}{10} = \tfrac{1}{10}$.
- $\tfrac{1}{4} \cdot \tfrac{1}{5} = \tfrac{1}{20}$.
- $\tfrac{1}{9} \cdot \tfrac{3}{10} = \tfrac{3}{90} = \tfrac{1}{30}$.
- $\tfrac{1}{16} \cdot \tfrac{2}{5} = \tfrac{2}{80} = \tfrac{1}{40}$.

Common denominator $120$:
$$\tfrac{12}{120} + \tfrac{6}{120} + \tfrac{4}{120} + \tfrac{3}{120} = \tfrac{25}{120} = \tfrac{5}{24}.$$

Therefore
$$\text{Var}!\left(\tfrac{1}{X}\right) = E!\left(\tfrac{1}{X^2}\right) - \left[E!\left(\tfrac{1}{X}\right)\right]^2 = \tfrac{5}{24} - \left(\tfrac{2}{5}\right)^2 = \tfrac{5}{24} - \tfrac{4}{25}.$$

Common denominator $600$:
$$= \tfrac{125}{600} - \tfrac{96}{600} = \tfrac{29}{600}.$$

$$\boxed{\text{Var}!\left(\tfrac{1}{X}\right) = \tfrac{29}{600} \approx 0.0483.}$$

Target: $\boxed{\text{Var}\!\left(\tfrac{1}{X}\right) = \tfrac{29}{600} \approx 0.0483.}$

STEP 4/5Part (d)

Strategy. Active recall: choose the method for Part (d) before unmasking. Reproduce exam-safe working, not just the final answer.

(d) Translate $Y < 20$: $\tfrac{30}{X} < 20 \iff X > \tfrac{30}{20} = 1.5$ (valid since $X > 0$). So ${Y < 20} = {X \in {2,3,4}}$.

Also ${X < 3} = {X \in {1,2}}$.

Intersection: ${X \in {2,3,4}} \cap {X \in {1,2}} = {X = 2}$.

$$P(X = 2) = \tfrac{1}{5} = \tfrac{2}{10}.$$
$$P(Y < 20) = P(X \in {2,3,4}) = \tfrac{2}{10} + \tfrac{3}{10} + \tfrac{4}{10} = \tfrac{9}{10}.$$
$$P(X<3 \mid Y<20) = \frac{P(X=2)}{P(Y<20)} = \frac{2/10}{9/10} = \boxed{\tfrac{2}{9}}.$$

Target: $\boxed{\tfrac{2}{9}}$

Formula anchor.

Key formula / concept

  • $E(g(X)) = \sum g(x) P(X=x)$.
  • $\text{Var}(X) = E(X^2) - [E(X)]^2$.
  • $E(aX) = aE(X)$, $\text{Var}(aX) = a^2 \text{Var}(X)$.
  • For conditional on a function of $X$: translate first to a condition on $X$ directly.
