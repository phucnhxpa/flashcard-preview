STEP 5/6Part (e)

(e) Two percentiles $\to$ two simultaneous equations.
- $P(Y < 152) = 0.05 \implies z_1 = -1.6449$.
- $P(Y > 180) = 0.40 \implies P(Y < 180) = 0.60 \implies z_2 = 0.2533$.

$$\frac{152 - \mu}{\sigma} = -1.6449 \qquad (1)$$
$$\frac{180 - \mu}{\sigma} = 0.2533 \qquad (2)$$

Subtract (1) from (2) to eliminate $\mu$:
$$\frac{180 - 152}{\sigma} = 0.2533 - (-1.6449)$$
$$\frac{28}{\sigma} = 1.8982.$$
$$\sigma = \frac{28}{1.8982} = 14.75 \approx \boxed{14.8\text{ g}}.$$

Back-substitute into (2):
$$\mu = 180 - 0.2533 \times 14.751 = 180 - 3.737 = \boxed{176.3\text{ g}}.$$
